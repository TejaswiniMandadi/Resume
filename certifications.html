  2. Agile software development

    October 2021 to Present

    Name:
    Agile software development

    Link: https://ccaps.umn.edu/agile-certificate/

    Summary:
    Gain a solid understanding of Agile philosophy and the supporting methodologies such as Scrum, Lean, extreme Programming, and Kanban. With Agile Certificate training courses participants will:

    • Take a deeper dive into how requirements and quality assurance are approached differently in Agile frameworks.
    • Understand the role of leadership in Agile, and the paradigm shift Agile brings for project vs. product, individual vs. team approach, and management vs. leading.
    • Examine the role of “Agile Coach,” the mindset, and the behaviors a coach models while serving a team.
    • Study advanced methods for teams, team dynamics, and what metrics can be used to help teams understand where they are in their Agile journey.
